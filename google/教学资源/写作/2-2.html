2-2​

作业讲解​

  • are at the same level
  • economic development has propelled1 xxx --随着经济的发展
  • nominalization 名词化
  • access superior medical resouces = seek professional help = see a doctor
  • escape the pressure from work
  • insufficient public funding ----不足的公共资金
  • traumatize 使受精神创伤

授课​

  • 思路:

    1. 开头段：背景+观点

    School education is the phase that mostly every child goes through, influencing one's future development to a great extent.

    1. 让步段: 说不支持的观点的好处

    2. 但是段: 支持观点的好处

  1. 本方观点段: 再说一个论点,说支持观点的好
  2. 结论段:
  • 雅思常见三种跑题模式
    1. 不能忽略动作主体
    2. should 是应当性 不能用措施证明,can 写措施
    3. 不能忽略限定词(限定目的)

词汇​

  • public investment
  • be of importance=important
  • out of xxx ---出于xxx目的
