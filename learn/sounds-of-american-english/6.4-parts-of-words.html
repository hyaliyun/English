6.4. 常见词根词缀

英文的词根大多来自拉丁语；而常用词缀，包括前缀和后缀 —— 它们都是表意的。

6.4.1. 常见前缀​

  1. un- (not, opposite of)
  • unhappy ʌnˈhæp.i
  • unknown ʌnˈnoʊn
  • unusual ʌnˈjuː.ʒu.əl
  1. re- (again, back)
  • redo riːˈduː
  • review rɪˈvjuː
  • return rɪˈtɝːn
  1. in- (not, into)
  • incomplete ˌɪn.kəmˈpliːt
  • invisible ɪnˈvɪz.ə.bəl
  • inside ɪnˈsaɪd
  1. dis- (not, opposite of)
  • disagree ˌdɪs.əˈɡriː
  • disappear ˌdɪs.əˈpɪr
  • disconnect ˌdɪs.kəˈnekt
  1. pre- (before)
  • preview ˈpriː.vjuː
  • predict prɪˈdɪkt
  • preschool ˈpriː.skuːl
  1. mis- (wrongly)
  • misunderstand ˌmɪs.ʌn.dɚˈstænd
  • misplace ˌmɪsˈpleɪs
  • mislead ˌmɪsˈliːd
  1. non- (not)
  • nonstick ˌnɑːnˈstɪk
  • nonprofit nɑnˈprɑf·ɪt
  • nonviolent ˌnɑːnˈvaɪə.lənt
  1. over- (too much)
  • overeat ˌoʊ.vɚˈiːt
  • overwork ˌoʊ.vɚˈwɝːk
  • overheat ˌoʊ.vɚˈhiːt
  1. sub- (under)
  • submarine ˌsʌb.məˈriːn
  • subtitle ˈsʌbˌtaɪ.t̬əl
  • subconscious ˌsʌbˈkɑːn.ʃəs
  1. inter- (between, among)
  • international ˌɪn.t̬ɚˈnæʃ.ən.əl
  • interact ˌɪn.t̬ɚˈækt
  • interrupt ˌɪn.t̬əˈrʌpt
  1. trans- (across, beyond)
  • transport ˈtræn.spɔːrt
  • transfer ˈtræns.fɝː
  • transform trænsˈfɔːrm
  1. under- (too little, below)
  • underpaid ˌʌn.dɚˈpeɪd
  • underestimate ˌʌn.dɚˈes.tə.meɪt
  • underground ˌʌn.dɚˈɡraʊnd
  1. super- (above, beyond)
  • superhuman ˌsuː.pɚˈhjuː.mən
  • supermarket ˈsuː.pɚˌmɑːr.kɪt
  • supervisor ˈsuː.pɚ.vaɪ.zɚ
  1. semi- (half, partly)
  • semicircle ˈsem.iˌsɝː.kəl
  • semifinal ˌsem.iˈfaɪ.nəl
  • semiconscious ˌsem.aɪ.ˈkɑːn.ʃəs/
  1. anti- (against)
  • antibiotic ˌæn.t̬i.baɪˈɑː.t̬ɪk
  • antifreeze ˈæn.t̬i.friːz
  • antisocial ˌæn.t̬iˈsoʊ.ʃəl
  1. de- (down, away from)
  • devalue ˌdiːˈvæl.juː
  • defrost ˌdiːˈfrɑːst
  • decode diːˈkoʊd
  1. en- (make, put into)
  • enable ɪˈneɪ.bəl
  • enrich ɪnˈrɪtʃ
  • encourage ɪnˈkɝː.ɪdʒ
  1. em- (cause to)
  • empower -ˈpaʊr
  • embrace ɪmˈbreɪs
  • employ ɪmˈplɔɪ
  1. non- (not)
  • nonexistent ˌnɑn·ɪɡˈzɪs·tənt
  • nonsense ˈnɑːn.sens
  • nonprofit nɑnˈprɑf·ɪt
  1. mid- (middle)
  • midnight ˈmɪd.naɪt
  • midterm ˈmɪd.tɝːm
  • midfield ˈmɪd.fiːld

  1. -able (capable of)

  • edible ˈed.ə.bəl
  1. -al (relating to)

  • musical ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl

  1. -ful (full of)
  • helpful ˈhelp.fəl
  • joyful ˈdʒɔɪ.fəl

  1. -less (without)
  • hopeless ˈhoʊp.ləs

  1. -ly (in the manner of)
  • quickly ˈkwɪk.li
  • slowly ˈsloʊ.li
  • happily ˈhæp.əl.i
  1. -ment (action or process)
  • development dɪˈvel.əp.mənt
  • agreement əˈɡriː.mənt
  • improvement ɪmˈpruːv.mənt
  1. -ness (state or quality)
  • kindness ˈkaɪnd.nəs
  • darkness ˈdɑːrk.nəs
  • happiness ˈhæp.i.nəs
  1. -er (one who, comparative)
  • teacher ˈtiː.tʃɚ
  • runner ˈrʌn.ɚ
  • faster fæst
  1. -or (one who)
  • actor ˈæk.tɚ
  • inventor ɪnˈven.t̬ɚ
  • conductor kənˈdʌk.tɚ
  1. -ist (one who practices)
  • artist ˈɑːr.t̬ɪst
  • scientist ˈsaɪən.tɪst
  • pianist ˈpiː.ən.ɪst
  1. -ion (action or condition)
  • celebration ˌsel.əˈbreɪ.ʃən
  • decision dɪˈsɪʒ.ən
  • creation kriˈeɪ.ʃən
  1. -tion (action or condition)
  • education ˌedʒ.əˈkeɪ.ʃən
  • suggestion səˈdʒes.tʃən
  • transportation ˌtræn.spɚˈteɪ.ʃən
  1. -sion (state or condition)
  • admission ədˈmɪʃ.ən
  • confusion kənˈfjuː.ʒən
  • extension ɪkˈsten.ʃən
  1. -ous (full of)
  • dangerous ˈdeɪn.dʒɚ.əs
  • nervous ˈnɝː.vəs
  • famous ˈfeɪ.məs
  1. -ive (tending to)
  • active ˈæk.tɪv
  • creative kriˈeɪ.t̬ɪv
  • attractive əˈtræk.tɪv
  1. -ic (relating to)
  • artistic ɑːrˈtɪs.tɪk
  • athletic æθˈlet̬.ɪk
  • energetic ˌen.ɚˈdʒet̬.ɪk
  1. -ity (state or quality)
  • reality riˈæl.ə.t̬i
  • ability əˈbɪl.ə.t̬i
  • simplicity sɪmˈplɪs.ə.t̬i
  1. -ance (state or quality)
  • performance pɚˈfɔːr.məns
  • importance ɪmˈpɔːr.təns
  • acceptance əkˈsep.təns
  1. -ence (state or quality)
  • difference ˈdɪf.ɚ.əns
  • independence ˌɪn.dɪˈpen.dəns
  • existence ɪɡˈzɪs.təns
